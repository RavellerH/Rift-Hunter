Gameplay

Key Features

A Monster Hunter-inspired loop built for solo play on desktop.

⚔️

Equipment-Driven Skills

No skill trees. Your armor and weapon define your abilities. Stack the same skill across multiple pieces to level it up — mix-sets are always viable.

🐉

Veilborn Hunting

Hunt ancient creatures called Veilborn across seven biomes. Every monster has weakpoints, breakable parts, elemental resistances, and an enrage phase.

🌌

Aerial Combat

Flying monsters pull you into a sky combat layer with reduced gravity. The Insect Glaive unlocks permanent aerial mode. Bow hunters dominate the skies.

🔨

Crafting from Parts

Every weapon and armor set is crafted from specific monster materials. You always know what you need and why. A full set takes 3–5 hunts, not 20.

🏰

Living Guild Hub

Veilwatch is a frontier outpost with named NPCs — a Guild Receptionist, Blacksmith, Chef, Elder Scholar, and more. Each has personality and lore to share.

🌍

Seven Biomes

Ancient Canopy, Wildspire Waste, Frosted Peaks, Coral Skyland, Volcanic Abyss, Rotten Hollow, and the Elder Sky Ruins — each with unique mechanics and monsters.

Lore

The World of Aethara

"The sky remembers what the earth has forgotten."

Three hundred years ago, an ancient civilization called the Aethori pushed through the membrane between their world and the Primordial Void. What came back was not them.

The creatures that followed — the Veilborn — are not evil. They are simply primordial. Vast, territorial, and ancient beyond reckoning. They reorganized the world around their territories, and humanity learned to survive alongside them.

You arrive at the frontier outpost of Veilwatch with a Rift Shard embedded in your palm. Someone is deliberately opening Rifts. The ancient balance is breaking.
