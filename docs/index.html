The World of Aethara

"The sky remembers what the earth has forgotten."

Three hundred years ago, an ancient civilization called the Aethori pushed through the membrane between their world and the Primordial Void. The creatures that followed — the Veilborn — are not evil. They are simply primordial. Impossibly old, and violently territorial.

You arrive at the frontier outpost of Veilwatch with a Rift Shard embedded in your palm. Rifts are opening where they shouldn't. The ancient balance is breaking. Someone is doing this deliberately.
